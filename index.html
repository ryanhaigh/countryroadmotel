Local Attractions

The authentic, gold rush city of Charters Towers sits amidst the beauty and sparseness of central north Queensland. Step back in time into a living museum. The spectacular buildings, the heritage pubs, the shops, museums and houses provide a treasure trove of experiences for visitors. Numerous tranquil water spots and secluded lava caves ensures fascinating walks.

Charters Towers is a perfect example of the real Australia, take a look around the town, enjoy a counter lunch, grab some takeaway or spoil yourself with an a la carte dinner as you watch the beatiful sunset. These are just some of the many activities available just minutes from the motel.

  • Enjoy the Charters Towers Country Music Festival - May
  • Visit one of the historic pubs
  • Taste Australian 'Bush Tucker'
  • Visit the Venus Battery - the last gold milling site in the North Queensland and fossick for gold
  • Visit Australia's first regional Stock Exchange, built in the 1870's
  • Look over the city from Rotary Lookout
  • Browse through Lissner Park
  • Stop off at the monument depicting Jupiter Mossman's gold discovery
